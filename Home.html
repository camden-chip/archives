Website Content Update

March 11, 2016

Archives New Zealand is currently making some changes to our website. This means pages you may have previously accessed could be missing.

Government Recordkeeping

If you are trying to access a page or document that relates to Government Recordkeeping it may now be located on the Records Toolkit as we are moving recordkeeping advice to the toolkit site. If you are not able to find the required information on the Records Toolkit please contact rkadvice@dia.govt.nz and a member of the Government Recordkeeping Team can provide you with the information and advice you require.

Research Services

If you are trying to access a page or document that relates to our Research Services and it has been removed please contact research.archives@dia.govt.nz and a Research Archivist will be able to assist you with your enquiry.

General Enquiries

If your enquiry does not relate to Government Recordkeeping or Research Services please email general.enquiries@dia.govt.nz for further assistance.

We appreciate your patience as we improve your online experience.
